About Us

Since 1940, McDonald's has grown from a small burger stand in California to a globally recognized brand, driven by our founder Ray Kroc's vision of serving quality food quickly with a smile. Our mission is to make delicious, feel-good moments easy for everyone, offering a diverse menu from classic burgers and fries to salads and smoothies.

We are committed to quality, exceptional service, and giving back to our communities through initiatives like the Ronald McDonald House Charities. Our sustainability efforts include responsible sourcing, waste reduction, and energy efficiency to positively impact the environment.
